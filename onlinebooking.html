The online booking form below allows you to forwarded the relevant information to the booking clerk.

The information will be used to pre-populate the booking form for your event.

This will be returned to you by email for checking and should be signed and returned to the address on the invoice that will also be attached.

You should include the booking fee with the forms unless you intend to pay by PayPal in which case you will be directed to our Payments page.

If you are having trouble completing the form or you have an error message returned when you submit it you can download the forms from our Downloads page.

Please note that payment by PayPal will incur additional charges. Please see our Fees & Charges page before proceeding.

Payment methods available are Cash, Cheque, Bank Transfer and PayPal (additional fees apply).

Please note we are not able to accept Debit or Credit card payments
